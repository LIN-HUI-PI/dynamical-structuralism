Dynamical Structuralism
動力結構主義

When a system fails, we intervene after the fact. This framework asks a different question: where, precisely, is the geometric position that precedes irreversibility? 當一個系統失控，我們通常在失控之後才介入。
這個框架問的是另一個問題：在失控成為不可逆之前，那個幾何位置在哪裡？

Three Claims · 三個主張
i.
Structure is not given. It is generated by tension dynamics. 結構不是給定的，它是張力場生成的。
ii.
The membrane is not a boundary. It is the shape produced when two tension fields make contact — it moves, it is never still. 膜不是邊界，它是兩個張力場接觸時產生的形狀——它在動，它不是靜止的線。
iii.
Intervention is not correcting deviation. It is rewriting rules at the geometric layer — while the system can still be rewritten. 介入不是修正偏差，而是在幾何層重寫規則——在系統還可以被重寫的時候。
Three Normative Principles · 三條規範原則
Principle of Intervention Point
The intervention point must be before irreversibility. 介入點必須在不可逆之前。
Principle of Restoration
Intervention may only restore the system's own geometry — not substitute an external standard for it. 介入只能恢復系統自己的幾何，不能用外部標準替換它。
Principle of Subject Confirmation
In any contact between person and system, the decision subject must be confirmable. 在人與系統的接觸中，決策主體必須可以被確認。
